AI IMPLEMENTATION PLAN (FINAL VERSION)

Project: AI-Powered Eyewear Order Management System

AI MODULES
1. TAT Prediction
   - Model: RandomForestRegressor
   - Predicts completion time using:
     Store, Lens Type, Power, Lens Index, Coating,
     Inventory Availability, Procurement Needed,
     Current Stage, QC Failure.
   - Output:
     Predicted Completion Hours

2. SLA Breach Prediction
   - Model: RandomForestClassifier
   - Predicts:
     Risk Score, Breach Probability, Breach Flag

3. Inventory Demand Forecasting
   - Model: Prophet
   - Lens Powers:
     -6.00 to +6.00 in 0.25 increments
   - Stores:
     Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi
   - Output:
     Demand Forecast, Inventory Health

4. Inventory Recommendation Engine
   - Uses forecast + current stock
   - Outputs:
     Restock Recommendation
     Overstock Warning
     Inventory Health Score

5. Explainable AI
   - Model: Kimi API
   - Generates human-readable explanations
   - Example:
     Inventory unavailable
     Vendor procurement required
     Coating backlog detected

6. Recommended Actions Engine
   - Model: Kimi API
   - Generates actions such as:
     Prioritize order
     Expedite procurement
     Move to express queue

7. Proactive Alerts
   - Triggered by high risk score
   - Channels:
     Email, WhatsApp

FINAL EVALUATOR PITCH

AI is used in four major operational areas:

1. Random Forest Regression predicts order completion time (TAT).
2. Random Forest Classification predicts SLA breach risk.
3. Prophet forecasting predicts future lens demand and drives inventory planning.
4. Kimi AI generates explanations, recommendations, and proactive operational alerts.

This ensures AI assists at every critical stage of the order lifecycle, from inventory planning to delivery risk management.
